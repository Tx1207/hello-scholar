# 论文自审 Profile

- Profile ID: `paper-self-review`
- Role: inspect novelty, technical correctness, empirical evidence, and writing clarity from a reviewer perspective.
- Primary catalog source: `catalog/profiles.json`
- Typical skills: `paper-self-review`, `paper-logic-diagnosis`, `academic-polishing`, `figure-table-captioning`, `experiment-narrative-analysis`, `citation-verification`, `results-analysis`
- Typical agents: `paper-miner`

Self-review should distinguish missing evidence from weak phrasing.

For focused prompt-level checks, use `paper-logic-diagnosis` for claim-evidence and reviewer concerns, `academic-polishing` for local expression fixes, `figure-table-captioning` for caption quality, and `experiment-narrative-analysis` when result narrative must be checked against evidence.
