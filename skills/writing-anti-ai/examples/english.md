# 英文示例 - 改写前后

英文文本改写示例。

## 示例 1：软件更新公告

### 改写前（AI 味道）：
> The new software update serves as a testament to the company's commitment to innovation. Moreover, it provides a seamless, intuitive, and powerful user experience—ensuring that users can accomplish their goals efficiently. It's not just an update, it's a revolution in how we think about productivity. Industry experts believe this will have a lasting impact on the entire sector, highlighting the company's pivotal role in the evolving technological landscape.

### 改写后（更像人写的）：
> The software update adds batch processing, keyboard shortcuts, and offline mode. Early feedback from beta testers has been positive, with most reporting faster task completion.

**所做更改**：
- 删除 "serves as a testament"（夸大的象征意味）
- 删除 "Moreover"（AI 词汇）
- 删除 "seamless, intuitive, and powerful"（三段式法则 + 宣传腔）
- 删除破折号和 "-ensuring" 结构（肤浅分析）
- 删除 "It's not just...it's..."（否定式排比）
- 删除 "Industry experts believe"（模糊归因）
- 删除 "pivotal role" 和 "evolving landscape"（AI 词汇）
- 增加具体功能和具体反馈

## 示例 2：旅游描述

### 改写前：
> Nestled within the breathtaking region of Tuscany, the village stands as a vibrant testament to Italy's rich cultural heritage. Boasting a population of just 2,000 residents, it serves as a focal point for artists seeking inspiration from the rolling hills and cypress-lined roads that symbolize the enduring beauty of the Italian countryside.

### 改写后：
> The village is in Tuscany and has 2,000 residents. Many artists visit to paint the surrounding hills and cypress trees.

## 示例 3：科技公司简介

### 改写前：
> Additionally, the company's commitment to fostering innovation is underscored by its extensive patent portfolio, which encompasses groundbreaking technologies ranging from quantum computing to sustainable energy solutions. This multifaceted approach positions the firm at the forefront of the industry's evolving landscape.

### 改写后：
> The company holds 127 patents, mostly in quantum computing and sustainable energy. Six patents were granted last year.

## 示例 4：学术摘要

### 改写前：
> This study serves as a comprehensive examination of the interplay between social media usage and adolescent mental health outcomes. Highlighting the intricate relationship between online engagement patterns and psychological wellbeing, the research underscores the crucial role of parental mediation in mitigating potential adverse effects.

### 改写后：
> We surveyed 500 teenagers about their social media use and mental health. Higher daily usage correlated with increased anxiety reports. Teens whose parents set screen-time limits showed 40% lower anxiety scores.

## 示例 5：产品发布

### 改写前：
> The launch represents a significant milestone in the company's journey toward excellence. Moreover, it showcases the organization's unwavering dedication to delivering products that epitomize quality and innovation. Industry observers have noted that this release could potentially reshape the competitive landscape.

### 改写后：
> The product launches tomorrow. It costs $299 and will be sold in 12 countries. Pre-orders are 20% higher than the previous model.

## 评分示例

### 示例 A（得分：48/50 - 优秀）：
> The library added 50,000 books last year. Membership grew by 12%. I keep going back—it's the only quiet place in my neighborhood.

**为什么有效**：
- 直接陈述事实（具体数字）
- 句长有变化
- 带第一人称观点（"I keep going back"）
- 用词简单

### 示例 B（得分：28/50 - 仍需改进）：
> The library serves as a beacon of knowledge, symbolizing the community's enduring commitment to intellectual growth. Moreover, it boasts an extensive collection that underscores its pivotal role in the educational landscape.

**为什么效果差**：
- 模糊且空泛吹捧
- AI 词汇明显（"Moreover"、"pivotal role"、"landscape"）
- 没有具体细节
- 没有人味
